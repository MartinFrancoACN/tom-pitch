Who We Target — And Why It Matters

Audience clarity drives creative relevance, bidding efficiency, and conversion quality. One message does not serve all.

01

In-Market Diners

UAE residents actively researching restaurants and dining experiences. Targeted via interest, behaviour, and lookalike signals on Meta and Search.

Goal →  Reservations
02

Experience Seekers

Lifestyle-led audiences drawn to cultural moments, chef-led events, and premium social occasions. Engaged through TikTok discovery and Meta Reels.

Goal →  Event Bookings & Footfall
03

Tourists & Visitors

International travellers and in-country visitors. Targeted via geo-signals, travel intent keywords, and tourism-adjacent placements.

Goal →  Footfall & Same-Day Visits
04

Corporate & Groups

Decision-makers booking private dining, group events, and corporate entertainment. Reached through LinkedIn-aligned Meta targeting and Search RFP terms.

Goal →  High-Value Group Enquiries

Abu Dhabi's audience is dual-layered — a stable local population and a constant flow of high-value international visitors. Always-on campaigns serve locals; tactical bursts capture tourists at peak intent moments.

Audience Layering
  • Segments are not siloed — they inform retargeting flows, creative versioning, and bid modifiers across all three channels
  • High creative volume and TikTok trend hijacking are tailored per segment to maximise native relevance

Attribution — 08 / 09

From Screen to Market — Closing the Attribution Loop

Digital impressions are only valuable when we can trace them to physical presence. We build attribution into every campaign from day one.

01

QR Codes

Campaign-specific QR codes embedded in ads, stories, and influencer content. Scanned on arrival to directly attribute visit to paid source.

02

Promo Codes & Exclusive Offers

Unique promo codes per campaign and per channel. Redeemed at point of sale to track media-to-visit conversion at SKU level.

03

Geo-Lift & Store Visit Signals

Meta and Google store visit measurement proxies. Geo-lift studies isolate incremental footfall driven by paid exposure vs organic.

04

Event RSVPs & Booking Confirmations

Tracked reservation and event RSVP flows as primary conversion events. Direct correlation between paid click and confirmed visit.

No siloed reporting. No wasted impressions. No guesswork.
